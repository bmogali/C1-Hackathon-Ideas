# Project Title: City Key & Venture Planner Ecosystem


**Tagline:** Moving Capital One from a *System of Record* to a *System of Experience* by owning the entire travel lifecycle.

### Executive Summary
> "Right now, traditional banks only own the transaction. We want Capital One to own the inspiration, the organization, and the execution of the entire travel journey. By pairing an interactive, collaborative travel planner with a gamified, real-time rewards layer called **City Key**, we transform our mobile app from a passive financial utility into an indispensable companion that enhances the actual vacation."

### The Problem
When cardholders travel, they frequently experience "rewards paralysis" or slip into card abandonment—shifting their spend to local cash, transit cards, or competitor cards with localized promotions. Because banks historically enter the picture only at the point of sale, they miss out on high-margin, pre-trip booking commissions and lose destination wallet share. 

### The Solution: A Dual-Engine System
Our solution captures 100% of the travel lifecycle through two modular, highly compatible features:

1. **The Venture Planner (Pre-Trip Inspiration):** A collaborative itinerary builder embedded directly within the Capital One app. As users map out their daily activities (e.g., museum visits, dining, tours), the app leverages predictive intent data to offer one-tap booking integration via Capital One Travel and Entertainment, capturing affiliate and booking revenue before the user ever leaves the app.
2. **City Key (On-Trip Execution):** A decoupled, transaction-driven gamification engine. It does not require a pre-built itinerary or battery-draining background GPS. Powered entirely by real-time authorization stream hooks, the moment a user card-swipes outside their home market, City Key activates. It turns tourism into a live game, offering dynamic "spend slabs" (milestones) that unlock localized, merchant-funded rewards (e.g., free coffee, museum discounts) as they spend.

### Business Value & Impact
* **Incremental Interchange Revenue:** By gamifying destination spend, we drive outsized cross-border and travel transaction volumes, which carry the highest interchange margins for issuers.
* **Unprecedented Intent Data:** Access to itinerary and real-time localized spending patterns provides Capital One with a massive data moat for hyper-personalized merchant cross-selling and advanced fraud mitigation.
* **Merchant-Funded Scalability:** Localized rewards are subsidized by merchant partners looking to capture high-spending Capital One tourists, minimizing internal rewards costs.

### Leading KPIs
* **Trip Share of Wallet (SoW):** Percentage of total trip cost (pre-trip booking + on-trip spend) captured on a Capital One card.
* **Destination Wallet Supremacy (DWS):** The ratio of a customer’s travel spending captured on the card versus their historical baseline spend in that specific destination tier.
